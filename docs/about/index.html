About Nick

I am 15 years old and a sophomore in high school. I am homeschooled and live with my family in St. Louis Missouri. I have 1 sister and 4 brothers, 2 of which are in college. Luckily I have 2 dogs that more than make up for their lost pressence. I have lots of interests, the biggest of which are reading, gaming, and programming.

Programming. I love programming. I think about the next thing I'm gonna make pretty much all day. My projects usually revolve around some concept that I find interesting as the creator, rather than actually making something the "best". Most of these projects are games. Like many my age, I was driven to learn to code in the pursuit of making games. As of writing this, I am attempting to deisgn a game which... I honestly don't even know how to describe. Mainly because I'm not even totally sure what it is supposed to be. To see more related to programming and game making, click here#.

Gaming. Most of the ideas for games to make come from other games that I play or have played. Sometimes just a tiny detail in a game acts as a catalyst for an entire game idea. Unfortunately, Almost none of these have been realized, and it's more than likely they wont be. This isn't necessarily a bad thing, just having a vague idea for a game does not mean you can/should actually make a game with it. Either way, I do think any good game maker should play games, a lot of them. A few of the games I have enjoyed the most over the years are Minecraft, Factorio, and Hollow Knight. I would not have anywhere enough space here to list all of my favorites, but these three each represent a different aspect of gaming I enjoy. If you're interested on reading more from myself about gaming and anything related, click here#.

Reading. I love to read. That includes novels and comics. I've been reading since I was young, much to my dad's delight. My dad is a book salesman and he always made us read, he would get mad at us for playing video games rather than reading, but that never really stopped us. I hope one day I can actually write good stories like the ones I read. At the moment I'm super into Star Wars books, mostly "legends" books, but I've liked most all I've read.
